## Notes:

1. **Hierarchy Levels**: Higher numbers = higher authority (10 = highest, 1 = lowest)
2. **Faculty-Level Positions**: Only have `faculty_id`, no `department_id`
3. **Department Positions**: Require both `faculty_id` and `department_id` (academic department)
4. **Office Positions**: Require only `department_id` (administrative department/office)
5. **Capacity**: Most positions = 1, but support positions may have multiple slots
